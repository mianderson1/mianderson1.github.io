PANDORA MEDIA

Decks + One Sheets

At Pandora, I lead trainings on Ad Ops processes for our teams in India, so I create a new Deck or One Sheet every week. As a principle, I strive for templatization when I create these documents: a layout that conforms to and expands upon our brand identity, and that's flexible and functional for educating my audience on a variety of topics. My presentations have ultimately reduced my team's workload by 50% in less than two years.
