DECKS

A keynote presentation for Initialized

THE CHALLENGE

Initialized needed 100 slides in two weeks for a keynote presentation at the Exploratorium, and 10 stakeholders across the company would offer critiques.

This project was new terrain for me and the brand: All prior presentations had been designed for the laptop. This presentation, however, would appear on a 16’x9’ screen in a large auditorium, which meant that I had to evolve the brand standards so they would support readability for audience members sitting 100 feet from the screen.

After many late nights, I produced a deck that uses color, type, and animation to create structure and an impactful narrative arc for every seat in the house.
