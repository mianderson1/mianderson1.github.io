BRIEF

How do you craft a fresh, modern aethetic for jewelry that conveys a sense of history and spirituality?

Sunday Tirio asked me to create a brand identity for her jewelry, which customers have often called "spiritual" in its form. I created a visual system that's bright and modern yet still appropriate for jewelry that resembles an artifact from another time.
