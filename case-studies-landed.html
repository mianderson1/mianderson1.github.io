Content Strategy

I executed on the design, editorial, and strategy for this marketing campaign. Marketing wanted to launch a promotion in order to drive more conversions, so I designed banner ads and a landing page to support that strategy. While the number of signups was short of our target, our series of content experiments (i.e. changes to the editorial framing, the position on the page, and more) gave us valuable data for future campaigns.
