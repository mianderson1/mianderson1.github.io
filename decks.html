B2B DECK FOR LANDED

One of Landed’s founders spoke at the Total Worker Health Symposium in October 2022, and he wanted our help selling the Landed brand and product to potential B2B partners. Alex wanted to demonstrate to attendees that our programs could lead directly to greater employee retention.

Landed’s director of B2B marketing wrote the copy. I edited that text and created all the visuals.

DECK TEMPLATE FOR LANDED EMPLOYEES

I created a Google Slides template for employees at Landed that was both visually beautiful and as flexible as possible: suitable for a number of use cases and users.

As this template was distributed around the company, I saw some of my designs replicated half a dozen times—therefore helping enforce a consistent brand identity even while my team of three was constrained by very limited resources.

Note: I’ve provided just three “chapters” from my template guide. More work is available upon request.
