BRAND DESIGN

Building a brand for Acaso, from art direction to final product in Webflow

THE CHALLENGE

My co-founders at Acaso needed a brand that would appeal to Gen Z fashion influencers: sophisticated and technical yet also hip and approachable.

My co-founders, Fernando and Camille, wanted to move their family business into the 21st century: Fernando's mom and aunt were seamstresses, and they created clothing for brands like Forever 21 at their family-owned factory in Brooklyn. Fernando and Camille saw an opportunity in the fashion influencers on TikTok: We would create their very own branded clothing; all the influencer would have to do is market the product to their following.

I created a brand from the ground up for this young, tech-savy audience: I set the visual standards and tone of voice, oversaw photoshoots and developed set-design concepts for our photography, and finally created Acasoâ€™s website in Figma and Webflow.
