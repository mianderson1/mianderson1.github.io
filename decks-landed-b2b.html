B2B Deck for Landed

BRIEF

Landed needed to convince potential employers that its programs could help with employee retention.

One of Landed’s founders spoke at the Total Worker Health Symposium in October 2022, and he wanted our help selling the Landed brand and product to potential B2B partners. Alex wanted to demonstrate to attendees that our programs could produce happier and healthier employees.

Landed’s director of B2B marketing wrote the copy. I edited that text and created all the visuals.
