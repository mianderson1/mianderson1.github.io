Studio Rosha

BRIEF

How do you make an interior designer's esoteric aesthetic universal?

I led the design and research for Studio Rosha’s new website and marketing plan. Jason Rosha, the practice’s principle designer, needed a new web presence that would balance his taste—more high-brow and esoteric—with the reality of his market: well-off professionals in the Bay Area who have more everyday sensibilities.
